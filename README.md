SpeedyAI ⚡️The Zero-Latency "Flow State" Tutor for the ADHD Mind🧠 The MissionSpeedyAI is a high-velocity, interactive learning engine built to bridge the "latency gap" in digital education. For students with ADHD, a 3-second "processing" pause isn't just a delay—it's a cognitive break that leads to disengagement.By leveraging Cerebras inference for near-instant response times, SpeedyAI transforms sluggish AI interactions into a high-energy, "FaceTime-like" experience that keeps pace with the speed of thought.⚡ Core Features🚀 Interruption-Based LearningTraditional bots require "turns." SpeedyAI allows students to interrupt mid-sentence. The AI pivots instantly, validating the new thought without a loading stutter.🌊 Flow-State UXWord-Burst Streaming: Phrases appear in clusters to match natural reading rhythms.Cognitive Anchoring: A persistent "Main Idea" card stays pinned so the student never loses the thread during tangents.Dopamine-Loop Feedback: Real-time micro-rewards and instant animations for correct answers.🎭 Adaptive PersonaThe tutor isn't a textbook; it's a "Genius Friend."
